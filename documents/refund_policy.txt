Refund and Return Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Eligibility for Refunds
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our software products and services. To be eligible for a refund, you must request it within 30 days of your purchase date.

2. How to Request a Refund
To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@company.com with your order number and reason for the refund request. Refund requests must be made in writing.

3. Processing Time
Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days and will be credited to your original payment method.

4. Non-Refundable Items
The following are not eligible for refunds:
- Custom development services after work has commenced
- Monthly subscription fees for services already rendered
- Products purchased during clearance sales

5. Technical Issues
If you're experiencing technical issues, we encourage you to contact our technical support team first. Many issues can be resolved without needing a refund.

6. Subscription Cancellations
You may cancel your monthly subscription at any time. The cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
